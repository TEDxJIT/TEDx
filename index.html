About the Institution

Jyothy Institute of Technology is a visionary institution, committed to fostering excellence in education and empowering students to lead with creativity and innovation. As a distinguished member of Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), it nurtures a culture of holistic learning and ethical values, shaping future leaders in engineering and technology.

Innovation for Masses

Welcome to a remarkable journey into the realm of "Innovation for Masses." Join us as we explore revolutionary ideas that empower communities and create a positive global impact. From cutting-edge technologies to transformative social initiatives, our visionary speakers will inspire you to believe in the potential of innovation for a better world. Together, we celebrate the trailblazers who dare to dream big and turn their ideas into reality, leaving no one behind in this pursuit of progress.

“Better to endure pain in an honorable manner than to seek joy in a shameful one.”

Massimo Pigliucci, from the TED Talk: Stoicism as a philosophy for an ordinary life

“NASA has this phrase that they like: ‘Failure is not an option.’ But failure has to be an option in art and in exploration, because it’s a leap of faith. And no important endeavor that required innovation was done without risk. You have to be willing to take those risks. So, that’s the thought I would leave you with, is that in whatever you’re doing, failure is an option, but fear is not.”

James Cameron, from the TED Talk: Before Avatar, a curious boy

All you need to do is to take 10 minutes out a day to step back, to familiarize yourself with the present moment so that you get to experience a greater sense of focus, calm, and clarity in your life.

Andy Puddicombe, from the TED Talk: All it takes is 10 mindful minutes

“I hope you remember that if you encounter an obstacle on the road, don’t think of it as an obstacle at all… think of it as a challenge to find a new path on the road less traveled.”

Lee Hyeon-seo, from the TED Talk: My escape from North Korea
